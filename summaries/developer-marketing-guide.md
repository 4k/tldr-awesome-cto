# Developer Marketing Guide

**Source:** https://www.devmarketingguide.com
**Section:** Marketing

---

## Key Insights

**Persona Definition Framework:**
- Answer "what problem does my product solve?" first
- Then identify who has that problem and seeks solutions
- Only build content after personas are defined, not before

**Newsletter Essentials:**
- Newsletter is minimum viable marketing - most direct/exclusive customer communication
- Start with manual drip campaigns to learn what works before automating
- Segment users based on content clicks, send more of what they engage with
- Setup DKIM, SPF, DMARC authentication to avoid spam filters

**Content Repurposing Strategy:**
- Takes 7 touchpoints for audience to respond to your message
- Convert single blog post into: tweets, slide deck, presentation, podcast, video
- Each format reaches different users in their preferred medium

**Blog Guidelines:**
- Only start if you can maintain consistency (even monthly posting works)
- Documentation pages often become most-visited content
- Focus links on your own content, not third-party sites

**Product Listing Sites:**
- List on StackShare, GetApp, Capterra, Product Hunt, Slant, Siftery, G2 Crowd
- Developers actively search these platforms for tools
- Provides backlinks for SEO and user review collection

**Email Best Practices:**
- Test emails with entire team before sending
- Use responsive templates
- Avoid spam trigger words in subject lines

## Bottom Line
Start with newsletters and product listings for immediate ROI, then repurpose single pieces of content across 7+ formats to maximize reach efficiency.

# The Atlassian Team Playbook

**Source:** https://www.atlassian.com/team-playbook
**Section:** Handbooks

---

## Key Insights
- Regular use of structured team practices improves performance by 15-20%
- Teams with clear goals are 4.5x more likely to collaborate effectively 
- Consistent planning/tracking methods free up 13% more time for core work
- **DACI Framework**: Driver (owns decision), Approver (makes final call), Contributors (provide input), Informed (need to know outcome)
- **Project Poster**: Single-page doc covering purpose, success metrics, timeline, and stakeholders
- **Network of Teams**: Map cross-team dependencies before project start to identify collaboration needs
- **Working Agreements**: Establish team norms upfront (communication preferences, meeting cadence, conflict resolution)
- **Page-Led Meetings**: Pre-write agenda with purpose, outcomes, and discussion points before meeting
- **User Manual**: Document for each team member covering work style, communication preferences, and collaboration needs
- **Weekly Team Updates**: Async status sharing format to reduce meeting overhead
- Implementation approach: Pick one practice → Follow structured template → Repeat until habitual

## Bottom Line
Atlassian's research shows that teams using structured frameworks (like DACI, OKRs, and working agreements) can boost performance 15-20% by creating clarity around goals, decisions, and collaboration norms.

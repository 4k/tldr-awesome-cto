# A Guide to Surviving Tech Due Diligence

**Source:** https://circleci.com/resources/tech-due-diligence/
**Section:** Due Diligence

---

## Key Insights
- **Pre-audit prep**: Prepare org charts, product/tech roadmaps, architecture diagrams, and complete data request documentation
- **Auditor interview focus areas**: Company culture assessment, technical debt evaluation, growth readiness capabilities
- **Data request optimization**: Structure responses to provide comprehensive product overview rather than fragmented answers
- **Mental preparation**: Frame as opportunity to showcase technical strengths vs. defensive exercise
- **Documentation completeness**: Having thorough documentation signals operational maturity to auditors

## Bottom Line
Success in tech due diligence comes down to comprehensive documentation prep and positioning technical debt/challenges as manageable rather than hiding them.

## Key Insights
- CTO salary/equity data available for 2009-2011 period with visualization tools
- Technical advisor is mandatory for web/mobile startups, not optional
- Equity-only CTO arrangements exist as viable option for early-stage companies
- CTO equity negotiations change significantly after funding rounds
- Part-time and consulting CTO models available for resource-constrained startups
- Different CTO roles exist: Founder CTO, Acting CTO, VP Engineering (distinct roles)
- Product/market fit timing affects developer hiring decisions
- Early employee equity frameworks documented with specific guidelines
- Free CTO consulting sessions offered as evaluation mechanism
